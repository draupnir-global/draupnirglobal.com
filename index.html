About

Built for generations

Draupnir Global GmbH is a Swiss holding company headquartered in Zug. We build and steward a portfolio of operating companies across precious metals, international trade, and digital assets — guided by patience, precision, and long-term vision.

Named after Draupnir, the golden ring of Odin that multiplied eightfold with every cycle, our structure is designed for compounding value across generations. We bring together deep industry expertise with the strategic discipline of Swiss corporate governance.
