ABOUT

Built for generations

Draupnir Global GmbH is a Swiss holding company headquartered in Lugano. We build and steward a portfolio of operating companies guided by patience, precision, and long-term vision.

Named after Draupnir, the golden ring of Odin that multiplied eightfold with every cycle, our structure is designed for compounding value across generations.

Swiss corporate governance applied with the discipline of long-term operators.

PHILOSOPHY

Forged, not assembled.

Long-term thinking

Swiss governance
